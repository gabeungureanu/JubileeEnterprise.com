About Jubilee Browser

Technology that honors Scripture, protects families, and serves the Church.

Our Mission

Jubilee Browser exists to help the Church navigate the digital world without losing its grounding in the Word of God.

We believe technology does not have to be neutral or exploitative to be effective. We're building modern, professional tools that honor Scripture, protect families, and serve the Church - demonstrating that faith and innovation can work together.

The Challenge We Address

The faith community faces persistent digital challenges that existing tools don't adequately address:

  • Exposure to harmful content - Pornography and inappropriate content remain one of the most persistent problems in Christian households, affecting children, teens, and adults alike.
  • Fragmented tools - Families and churches juggle separate browsers, Bible apps, and filtering software, creating complexity and gaps in protection.
  • Secular digital environments - Most technology is driven by ads, data harvesting, and engagement manipulation, creating hostile environments for spiritual growth.
  • Technical burden - Churches and schools often lack dedicated IT resources to manage device policies, filtering systems, and browser configurations.

Our Approach

Jubilee Browser brings together safety, clarity, and biblical grounding in a single, trusted application.

Protection by Design

Content filtering is built into the browser and cannot be bypassed. This removes the burden from parents, pastors, and IT teams who struggle to maintain protection across multiple devices.

Clear Separation

A visible switch between the public internet and the Jubilee Bible Internet helps users understand when they're in a Scripture-centered environment, reinforcing intentional time in God's Word.

Scripture at the Center

Unlike browsers that treat the Bible as just another website, Jubilee Browser places Scripture at the center of the experience - accessed directly without distractions or algorithm-driven content.

One Tool, Not Many

Instead of managing a browser, Bible app, filtering software, and study tools separately, users install one trusted application that handles all of these functions.

Our Values

Scripture-Centered

Everything we build starts with the question: does this help people engage with God's Word? The Bible isn't an add-on feature - it's the foundation of what we do.

Family Protection

We take seriously our responsibility to protect families. Our content filtering cannot be disabled because we believe protection should be the default, not an option that can be circumvented.

Privacy Respected

We don't collect your browsing history, search queries, or personal data. We don't show ads or sell information. Your browsing stays on your computer where it belongs.

Accessible to All

Jubilee Browser is free because we believe technology that serves the Church should be accessible to everyone - from large churches to small home groups, from schools to individual families.

Sustainable Stewardship

Our funding model is rooted in stewardship rather than advertising or data monetization. We keep costs low, participation optional, and never pressure users to pay for what they don't need.

Looking Forward

Jubilee Browser is not just a product - it's infrastructure for the future of faith-based technology. We're building a foundation for tools that serve the Church for generations to come:

  • Advanced Bible study environments
  • Prayer networks and ministry collaboration spaces
  • Educational resources for Christian schools
  • Discipleship and community formation tools
  • Trusted digital identity for churches and ministries

By adopting Jubilee Browser today, churches and families position themselves at the forefront of faithful digital innovation.

In Summary

Jubilee Browser offers the faith-based community a rare combination of safety, simplicity, Scripture-centered design, and long-term vision. It replaces fragmented tools with a single, trusted platform, protects families from harmful content, supports churches in their mission, and creates a sustainable digital ecosystem aligned with biblical values.

Jubilee Browser helps the Church navigate the digital world without losing its grounding in the Word of God.
